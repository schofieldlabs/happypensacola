Important Notary Public Disclosure

**Ralph H. Schofield, Jr.** is a Florida Notary Public, Commission # HH 645857.

A notary public's duty is to ensure the identity of signers and witness the signing of documents. A notary public is prohibited by law from preparing legal documents or giving legal advice.
