A Foundation Built on Expertise & Passion

Jessica Wade: A Decade of Wellness

For over a decade, **Jessica Wade** has been a cornerstone of personalized wellness in Pensacola. As the longtime owner of **Happy Pilates, LLC**, she has dedicated her career to guiding individuals toward enhanced physical and mental well-being.

Her extensive certifications in Pilates, Yoga, and Gyrotonic reflect a deep commitment to diverse movement modalities, offering tailored programs that empower clients to achieve their unique health goals. Jessica's studio has been a sanctuary for mindful movement, building a legacy of healthier, happier lives.

Ralph H. Schofield, Jr.: Diverse Expertise & Service

**Ralph H. Schofield, Jr.** brings a multifaceted background rooted in his existing business, **Schofield Collaborative, LLC**. His professional journey encompasses law, dispute resolution, and spiritual guidance.

As a Florida Supreme Court Certified Mediator and Qualified Arbitrator, along with being a FINRA Approved Arbitrator, Ralph has committed to fostering amicable resolutions. His role as an ordained minister and Florida Notary Public further expands his capacity to serve the community, whether through ceremonial services, public speaking, or official document notarization. Ralph's diverse expertise is united by a passion for facilitating positive outcomes and offering comprehensive support.

The Birth of Happy Pensacola (Est. 2025)

Born from a shared vision for community betterment, **Happy Pensacola was established in 2025** to bring together Jessica and Ralph's individual strengths under one unified, community-oriented umbrella.

Our goal is to provide a comprehensive range of services—from holistic wellness and professional development to dispute resolution and essential legal and spiritual support—all designed to enrich the lives of individuals and families in the Pensacola area. Happy Pensacola is more than just a business; it's a commitment to fostering a stronger, happier, and more harmonious community. We believe in empowering our neighbors through accessible, high-quality resources and compassionate guidance.

Our Mission

To enhance the well-being and prosperity of the Pensacola community by offering expert, diverse, and accessible services rooted in professionalism, integrity, and a genuine commitment to positive outcomes.
